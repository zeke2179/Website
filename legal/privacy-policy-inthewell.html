Privacy Policy

This policy applies to In The Well by Zeke Digital Solutions.

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Overview

In The Well is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our fishing catch tracking app handles your information.

Data Collection

In The Well stores fishing trip, fisherman, fish species, catch count, subscription status, Siri shortcut, and Apple Watch sync data locally on your devices using Apple system services. Fishing trip data you enter is not sent to developer-operated servers. The information you create may include:

  • Trip records and dates
  • Angler names and information
  • Fish species and catch records
  • Catch counts and daily limit tracking
  • Trip statistics and historical data
  • User preferences and settings

This information is stored locally on your devices using Apple system services.

Data Storage

  • Fishing trip data is not sent to developer-operated servers
  • Active trip data may sync between your iPhone and Apple Watch through Apple's WatchConnectivity framework
  • Subscription purchases, product loading, entitlement checks, and purchase restoration are handled by Apple through StoreKit
  • The app may contact a remote configuration file to check the minimum supported app version and show an update prompt when needed
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks have access to your data

Siri Integration

In The Well supports Siri voice commands for hands-free catch logging. When you use Siri integration:

  • Your voice commands are processed locally on your device
  • The app does not store audio recordings or send them to developer-operated servers
  • Siri's privacy policy applies to voice processing (follow Apple's Siri Privacy guidelines)
  • All resulting data remains stored locally on your device

Apple Services

In The Well uses Apple system services for Siri voice commands, Apple Watch syncing, and subscriptions. Apple's privacy terms may apply when you use those services.

Data Security

In The Well does not implement additional security measures beyond your device's built-in security features. All data is stored locally on your device. The security of your information depends entirely on your device's built-in security features and your personal device management practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of In The Well following the posting of a revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.
