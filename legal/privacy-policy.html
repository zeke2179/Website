Privacy Policy for In The Well

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

In The Well is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our fishing catch tracking app handles your information.

Data Collection

In The Well does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your device. All fishing trip data, including:

  • Trip records and dates
  • Angler names and information
  • Fish species and catch records
  • Catch counts and daily limit tracking
  • Trip statistics and historical data
  • User preferences and settings

are stored exclusively and locally on your iOS device only.

Data Storage

  • All data remains on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is shared with any third parties
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks have access to your data
  • No cloud backup or synchronization occurs

Siri Integration

In The Well supports Siri voice commands for hands-free catch logging. When you use Siri integration:

  • Your voice commands are processed locally on your device
  • No audio recordings are sent to external servers
  • Siri's privacy policy applies to voice processing (follow Apple's Siri Privacy guidelines)
  • All resulting data remains stored locally on your device

Permissions

All permissions requested by In The Well are optional and the app will function without granting them. Permissions may include calendar or reminder access for trip management, but are not required.

Data Security

In The Well does not implement additional security measures beyond your device's built-in security features. All data is stored locally on your device. The security of your information depends entirely on your device's built-in security features and your personal device management practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of In The Well following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.
