Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Zeke Digital Solutions manages privacy across our apps and services.

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Zeke Digital Solutions builds mobile and web apps with privacy in mind. This page explains our general privacy approach, while each app may have its own specific policy details.

General Data Handling Principles

We strive to minimize data collection and keep app data local whenever possible. For apps that do not require external servers, data remains on your device only.

When an app does use cloud services, analytics, or third-party integrations, those details are described in the app-specific privacy policy.

Data Storage

The default behavior for Zeke Digital Solutions apps is:

  • Prefer local storage on the user's device
  • No data is shared with third parties unless explicitly stated
  • No analytics or advertising tracking is used unless disclosed
  • No automatic cloud synchronization without user consent

Permissions

Permissions requested by our apps are intended to support app functionality. Each app will explain why a permission is requested and whether it is optional.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our apps after updates means you accept the revised policy.
